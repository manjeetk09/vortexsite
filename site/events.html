Google Whack

All you have to do is surf the web, literally! Well, it’s not as easy as that sounds. A Googlewhack is a type of contest for finding a Google search query consisting of exactly two words without quotation marks that returns exactly one hit. A Googlewhack must consist of two actual words found in a dictionary. A Googlewhack is considered legitimate if both of the searched-for words appear in the result page. You just need to be a Netizen. Little or more knowledge about Internet and Google or any search engine is the only requirement. Take this opportunity to prove your mettle and show-off the depth of your knowledge of World Wide Web. More Details
