Guest Lectures

  • Manish Sisodia

    Manish Sisodia: The guy who journeyed from his humble beginnings as a son of a teacher to become the deputy chief minister of Delhi, Manish Sisodia has shown thousands the way of life. Along with Arvind Kejriwal and Abhinandan Sekhri, Sisodia was a founder of the Public Cause Research Foundation in 2006. He was also a key figure in Kabir, a non-governmental organisation. From being an activist, fighting for people's rights to featuring in 100 most influential Indians 2016 by The Indian Express, he has established himself as a man with principles and with the perseverance and passion to fight for people.

  • Diwakar Vaish: the mind behind Manav, India's first completely indigenous 3D printed humanoid robot, Head of Robotics and Research at A-SET Training and Research Institutes, and an innovator at heart, Diwakar Vaish's vision and talent can't be summed be up in a few lines. His achievements have been recognized by former president of India Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam & Smt. Pratibha Patil. Vaish has given 2 ted talks and serves as an inspiration to thousands of young minds to pursue their passion in robotics.

  • Anurag Batra


    Anurag Batra is editor-in-chief of the indian business magazine BW Businessworld. BW Businessworld is one of the oldest as well as most respected business publications in the country. The magazine was born in 1981, and over the past three decades, it has become known for its rigorously reported and deeply analysed reports and articles on the economy, banking, finance, industries, corporate houses and entrepreneurs. It has become an essential decision making tool for leaders both in the government as well as in corporate houses. Batra has been an integral part of the magazine's journey to fame and recognition.

  • Pavan Duggal


    Pavan Duggal is an advocate specialized in the field of Cyberlaw, E-Commerce law. He is also a member of NOMCOM Committee on Multilingual Internet Names Consortium(MINC). He was the Conference Director of the International Conference on Cyberlaw & Cybercrime organized by Cyberlaws.Net. In his blog entitled “Cyber Laws in Today’s Times” at The Economic Times, he has written on different aspects and issues pertaining to Cyberlaw. He has written several books on various diverse and complex aspects concerning the legalities of policy related issues impacting cyberspace, Internet and the World Wide Web. He is regarded by many as an expert on issues pertaining to Cyber laws.

  • Dilip Chenoy


    Dilip chenoy is fomer Managing Director & CEO of National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC). Chenoy spearheaded NSDC for 5 years which aims to skill 150 million Indians by 2022 and hence contributed in harnessing India’s demographic dividend. Prior to NSDC, he was associated with Confederation of Indian Industry (CII) for 19 years and served as the Regional Director for Southern region. In 2004, he joined Society of Indian Automobile Manufacturers (SIAM) as Director General. A true visionary at heart, Chenoy has served as a ray of hope for thousands of people to skillfully improve their lives.
